School Of Ministry

Course description

To equip individuals in the Word of God and to disciple them. .

Course requirements

  • Grade 8 Certificate.
  • Minimum age requirement - 16 years old.
  • Certificate Program - No minimum qualification prerequisites
  • Diploma Program - Certificate in Ministry.
  • Bachelor Program - Diploma in Ministry.

High Hope University Campus does not have a prescribed academic year. Students may start their studies at any time of the year. You have the opportunity to join the Bible School whenever you decide to start.

All our candidates are requested to make advance payment in full before the course commences.

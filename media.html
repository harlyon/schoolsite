Course description

Media Studies equips students with the critical and analytical skills to be able to operate in the 21st Century media environment and the Information Age.Â Our Media Studies major would be useful for people who would like to be in Web Development, Sound Recording, Sound Engineering field. Majoring in Media Studies will unlock your creative potential and help you develop practical skills.

Course requirements

  • Grade 12 Certificate.
  • Mathematics or Mathematical Literacy.
  • Mature age applicants, 45 years and older without a school leaving qualification, may apply for conditional exemption.
  • Foreign Students whose first language is not English, may be required to provide proof of proficiency in English prior to admission to the programme.

High Hope University Campus does not have a prescribed academic year. Students may start their studies at any time of the year. You have the opportunity to join the Bible School whenever you decide to start.

All our candidates are requested to make advance payment in full before the course commences.

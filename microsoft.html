Microsoft Office

Course description

Microsoft Office training courses suitable for anyone wanting to improve their computer skills, or learn to use a computer. All versions of Windows and MS Office available.  Beginners to Advanced Levels in Windows, Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint, Access, Internet, Publisher, MS Project, Touch Typing .The Microsoft Office programme is designed to equip you with the skills needed to create Word documents, formulate data, produce presentations and connect with other people. Enrol and equip yourself with the skills necessary to take full advantage of Microsoft Office features..

Course requirements

  • Grade 12 Certificate.
  • Mathematics or Mathematical Literacy.
  • Mature age applicants, 45 years and older without a school leaving qualification, may apply for conditional exemption.
  • Foreign Students whose first language is not English, may be required to provide proof of proficiency in English prior to admission to the programme.

High Hope University Campus does not have a prescribed academic year. Students may start their studies at any time of the year. You have the opportunity to join the Bible School whenever you decide to start.

All our candidates are requested to make advance payment in full before the course commences.
